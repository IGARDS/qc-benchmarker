Documentation


QC Benchmarker relies on using data generated from a commercial protein digest, and that the data be acquired in a standardized fashion.
While still in beta, only a HeLa digest is supported, but in the future the following will be supported:
Thermo Scientific HeLa Digest
Promega Yeast Digest
Waters E. coli Digest

The recommended loading is 200 ng on column. Prepare the material using the manufacturers suggested protocol.
The recommended LC gradient for HeLa and Yeast is: (if using a trap/elute set-up, the first step is 10 minutes of trapping)

Time (min) % Acetonitrile
0 5
10 5
115 22
130 32
140 95
150 95
150.1 5
160 5

There will be a shorter gradient used for E. coli or for ~40 ng loading of HeLa and yeast.
