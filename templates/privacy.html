QC Benchmarker is not recording the IP address or any other information to track your computer. We are aware that there can be identifiable information in some raw data files (such as Thermo's .raw), and have taken steps to insure identifiable data is not retained on the server. The first processing step that occurs once the raw file(s) has been uploaded is that it is converted to .mzML, which no longer has the instrument serial number or local directory structure. The original raw file(s) is kept for no longer than one week on the server, at which point it is removed.
